Taraweeh Prayer at the Masjid Announcement

Taraweeh prayers will be held at the Central Masjid following Isha prayer. The prayer will commence 20 minutes after the Adhan of Isha, starting with the Isha prayer, followed by Taraweeh. All worshippers are encouraged to attend and participate in the congregational prayers.

Weekly Halaqah for men and women

Quran Halaqah for both men and women shall take place in the masjid over the weekend throughout Ramadan. Please do join and participate in reciting the Holy Quran together with fellow brothers or sisters.

Timings for men: Sunday 13:00 - 14:00

Timings for women: Saturday 12:00 - 13:30

Sunday Iftars organized by PSIY

Every Sunday throughout Ramadan, PSIY will organise Iftars at Raatin Nuoristalo and everyone is invited.
