Quran Classes at Oulu Central Mosque

Every Saturday from 13:30 to 15:30

Kids are divided into 4 different classes (age group 4+).

Class 1: Beginners 1

  • Memorization from Surah-Naas onwards (last 14 surahs of the Quran)
  • Learning the Arabic letters

Class 2: Beginners 2

  • Memorization from Surah Al-Zalzala (number 99) onwards
  • Learning the sound of Arabic letters with harkat (Arabic vowels) and other basics of the Arabic language

Class 3: Intermediate 1

  • Reading and memorization from Surah-Naba onwards
  • Tajweed rules and implementation

Class 4: Intermediate 2

  • Reading and memorization from Surah Mujaadila onwards
  • Tajweed rules and implementation

To enrol your child, you can come to the masjid during the specified time and contact the Quran teacher for the enrolment.
