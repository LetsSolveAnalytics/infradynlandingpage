Join the PSIY Community

Any Muslim can join the PSIY community by filling out the membership forms on this page, regardless of their background or where they live in Finland. (The form can be found on this page and printed at the entrance of the mosque)

Two-step joining process:

Step 1: Each adult family member fills out a separate form:

  • Children under the age of 18 must be added to either form - either the father's or the mother's form.
  • Association membership form

Note: Children aged 12-17 sign an additional attachment:

  • Attachment link

Step 2: After completing the form, return it to PSIY using one of the methods below:

  • Send it to the box at the bottom of the page
  • Send it by email: info@psiy.fi
  • Bring it to the Yusuf An-Naaghi Mosque and put it in the membership box (next to the entrance).
  • Mail to: Linnankatu 23-25, 90100 Oulu
